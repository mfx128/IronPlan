Iron Plan

CNS Priming: Flood muscles with blood and lubricate joints without fatigue.
Core Bonus: Add Planks or Bird-Dogs to any warmup for stability.

Cardio: 5-10 Min light (Walk or Bike)
Mobility: Dynamic leg swings, walking lunges, bodyweight squats (2×15). Hip Openers: Pigeon pose (30s) or hip flexor stretch.
Acclimation: Bodyweight glute bridges, ankle mobility drills.
Cardio: 5-10 Min Rower or Jump Rope
Mobility: Shoulder arm circles, band pull-aparts (2×15). Injury Prev: Wall slides (2×15).
Acclimation: Light warm-up sets (pushups, light rows) + 2×15 External Rotations.
Cardio: 5-10 Min light cardio
Mobility: Arm circles, Y-Raises (15 reps), band dislocates (2×15).
Acclimation: Light sets of overhead presses and lateral raises. Accessory: 2-3 sets YTWLs.
Cardio: 5-10 Min Treadmill Walk
Mobility: Hip circles, leg swings, bodyweight RDLs (2×15).
Acclimation: Glute bridges, bird-dogs. Balance: Single-leg RDLs (10/leg).
Movement Prep: 5 Min easy walk + full-body dynamic stretch — leg swings, arm circles, torso twists (10 reps each).
Intent: Active recovery day. Light enough to flush soreness — not a punishment session. Keep intensity conversational.
Note: No warm-up sets needed. Let your body guide the pace today.

Rest Guide: Density (High rep): 30-60s. Hypertrophy (Mod rep): 60-90s. Strength (Low rep/Heavy): 2-3 min. Supersets/Drop sets: 15-30s between exercises.
