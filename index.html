Iron Plan

CNS Priming: Flood muscles with blood and lubricate joints without fatigue.
Core Bonus: Add Planks or Bird-Dogs to any warmup for stability.

Cardio: 5-10 Min light (Walk or Bike)
Mobility: Dynamic leg swings, walking lunges, bodyweight squats (2x15). Hip Openers: Pigeon pose (30s) or hip flexor stretch.
Acclimation: Bodyweight glute bridges, ankle mobility drills.
Cardio: 5-10 Min Rower or Jump Rope
Mobility: Shoulder arm circles, band pull-aparts (2x15). Injury Prev: Wall slides (2x15).
Acclimation: Light warm-up sets (pushups, light rows) + 2x15 External Rotations.
Cardio: 5-10 Min light cardio
Mobility: Arm circles, Y-Raises (15 reps), band dislocates (2x15).
Acclimation: Light sets of overhead presses and lateral raises. Accessory: 2-3 sets YTWLs.
Cardio: 5-10 Min Treadmill Walk

Acclimation: Glute bridges, bird-dogs. Balance: Single-leg RDLs (10/leg).
Cardio: 5-10 Min light cardio
Mobility: Band pull-aparts, arm circles (2x15). Injury Prev: External Rotations (2x15).
Acclimation: Light warm-up sets for pressing movements.
Cardio: 5-10 Min Rower or light cardio
Mobility: Scapular pull-ups, band pull-aparts (2x15).
Acclimation: Light sets of pulldowns or rows. Accessory: YTWLs.

Rest Guide: Density (High rep): 30-60s. Hypertrophy (Mod rep): 60-90s. Strength (Low rep/Heavy): 2-3 mins. Supersets/Drop sets: 15-30s.
